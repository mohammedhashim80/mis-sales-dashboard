# 📊 Company MIS Dashboard — Automated Sales Reporting System

**Tools:** Excel · Power BI · DAX · Power Query  
**Domain:** Sales & Revenue Management  
**Region:** UAE & KSA  

---

## 🧾 Project Overview

Designed and built a complete **Monthly MIS Reporting System** for a trading company operating across UAE and KSA. The system automates data aggregation, KPI tracking, and executive reporting — replacing manual processes and saving 5+ hours per reporting cycle.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

The company had 240+ monthly sales transactions across 5 regions and 5 product categories with no centralized reporting system. Management relied on manual Excel files with no KPI visibility, no trend analysis, and no rep-level performance tracking.

---

## ✅ Solution Built

### 📁 Excel MIS Workbook (3 Sheets)

| Sheet | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Raw Sales Data** | 240 transactions with 14 columns — region, category, rep, channel, revenue, cost, margin |
| **MIS Dashboard** | Auto-calculated KPIs, monthly revenue trend, regional & category breakdown |
| **Rep Performance** | Individual sales rep analysis with performance classification |

### 📊 Power BI Dashboard (5 Pages)
- **Executive Summary** — Total revenue, profit, margin KPIs
- **Monthly Trend** — Revenue vs Target line chart
- **Regional Analysis** — Map + bar chart by UAE/KSA region
- **Category Breakdown** — Treemap + table by product category
- **Rep Scorecard** — Ranked performance table with status flags

---

## 📈 Key KPIs Tracked

- ✅ Total Revenue vs Target (Achievement %)
- ✅ Gross Profit & Gross Margin %
- ✅ Monthly Revenue Trend (Jan–Dec)
- ✅ Regional Revenue Breakdown (Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, Riyadh, Jeddah)
- ✅ Category Performance (Electronics, Furniture, Clothing, F&B, Automotive)
- ✅ Sales Rep Scorecard with performance flags

---

## 💡 Key Insights Generated

- Identified top-performing region contributing highest revenue
- Flagged 2 sales reps below 30% margin threshold
- Detected Q3 revenue dip — recommended promotional push
- Electronics category showed highest gross margin at ~38%

---

## 🛠️ Technical Highlights

- **240 rows** of realistic transaction data across 12 months
- **SUMIF / COUNTIF / AVERAGEIF** formulas for dynamic aggregation
- **Conditional formatting** for KPI status (On Track / Below Target)
- **Power BI DAX measures** for YTD, MoM growth, and margin calculations
- **No macros required** — fully formula-driven, easy to update monthly

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Download `MIS_Sales_Report_Mohammed_Hashim.xlsx`
2. Open in Microsoft Excel 2016 or later
3. Navigate to **MIS Dashboard** sheet for summary KPIs
4. Update **Raw Sales Data** with new transactions — all formulas auto-refresh
5. Open `MIS_Dashboard.pbix` in Power BI Desktop for visual dashboard
