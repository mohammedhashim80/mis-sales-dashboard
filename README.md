# Company MIS Dashboard — Automated Sales Reporting System

*Tools:* Excel · Power BI · DAX · Power Query
*Domain:* Sales & Revenue Management
*Region:* UAE & KSA
*Author:* Mohammed Hashim — Data Analyst | Dubai

---

## Project Overview

Built a complete Monthly MIS Reporting System for a trading company 
operating across UAE and KSA. The system automates data aggregation, 
KPI tracking, and executive reporting — replacing manual processes 
and saving 5+ hours per reporting cycle.

---

## Business Problem

The company had 240+ monthly sales transactions across 5 regions 
and 5 product categories with no centralized reporting system. 
Management relied on manual Excel files with no KPI visibility, 
no trend analysis, and no rep-level performance tracking.

---

## Solution Built

### Excel MIS Workbook — 3 Sheets

| Sheet | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Raw Sales Data | 240 transactions — region, category, rep, revenue, cost, margin |
| MIS Dashboard | Auto KPIs, monthly trend, regional and category breakdown |
| Rep Performance | Individual scorecard with performance classification |

### Key KPIs Tracked

- Total Revenue vs Target (Achievement %)
- Gross Profit and Gross Margin %
- Monthly Revenue Trend (Jan to Dec)
- Regional Breakdown — Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, Riyadh, Jeddah
- Category Performance — Electronics, Furniture, Clothing, F&B, Automotive
- Sales Rep Scorecard with performance flags

---

## Key Insights Generated

- Identified top-performing region contributing highest revenue
- Flagged 2 sales reps below 30% margin threshold
- Detected Q3 revenue dip and recommended promotional push
- Electronics category showed highest gross margin at 38%

---

## Technical Highlights

- 240 rows of realistic transaction data across 12 months
- SUMIF, COUNTIF, AVERAGEIF formulas for dynamic aggregation
- Conditional formatting for KPI status flags
- No macros required — fully formula-driven, easy to update monthly
